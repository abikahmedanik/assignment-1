Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Innovation is creating or
improving products, processes, or ideas,
driving progress, solving problems,
enhancing efficiency, and transforming
industries for betterment.

Sustainability

Sustainability focuses on
meeting present needs while preserving
resources, promoting environmental health,
social equity, and economic stability
for future generations.

Infrastructure

Infrastructure refers to
fundamental facilities and systems
like roads, bridges, water supply,
power, and communication networks
that support societal and economic activities.

Empowerment

Empowerment is enabling individuals
or groups with knowledge, skills,
and resources to make decisions,
take actions, and achieve their potential.

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Green Energy

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future.

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.
